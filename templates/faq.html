Frequently asked Questions

How Do I Search For a Pet?

furrypaws.com is a searchable list of pets from our shelter. To find an adoptable pet, you can begin your search using “Find a Pet” located above. Since Petfinder is updated regularly, we recommend that you keep checking back or create a saved search email alert to be notified when new pets matching your search criteria are added to the site.

Can I Save My Search or Be Notified When New Pets Are Available?

To be notified by email when pets matching your search criteria are first posted on the site, use our saved search feature. After performing a search, you will see a button with a bell icon to the right of your search results. Click on this button to set an email alert for your search.
